Day One

Every great story has a first page. This is ours.

"Not all those who wander are lost — but I'm glad I wandered right to you."

Today marks the beginning of a year of little letters, memories preserved in amber, and all the small beautiful things I don't want to forget.

Each day you open this, something new will be waiting. Some pages will be silly. Some will be sentimental. All of them will be true.

Welcome to our year. I love you.
